## What Data Analysis Skills Can You Gain with SPSS Program Training?

### Basic Statistical Concepts and Data Entry Operations in SPSS

- **Data entry and editing:** You learn to create a data set in SPSS and define variables.
- **Descriptive statistics:** You can calculate basic statistical measures such as mean, median, mode, standard deviation.
- **Data cleaning:** You learn methods to manage missing data and eliminate outliers.

### Hypothesis Testing and Statistical Analyses with SPSS

Although not limited to the following, frequently used tests:

- **T-Test:** Used to analyze differences between two groups.
- **ANOVA:** Preferred for comparing three or more groups.
- **Regression analysis:** Applied to determine the relationship between dependent and independent variables.

### Graphs and Data Visualization Techniques Using SPSS

Although not limited to the following, frequently used graphs:

- **Histogram and distribution graphs:** Used to visualize the distribution of data.
- **Box plots (Boxplot):** An ideal method for detecting outliers.
- **Correlation matrix:** Helps understand relationships between variables.
